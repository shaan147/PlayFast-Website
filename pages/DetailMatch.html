How does it work?

  • You arrive at the site before 15 minutes
  • The host divides into two teams
  • Each person will play as a goalkeeper once
  • Teams will be swapped to make a balance

What is Play Fast?

  • Play Fast is a community of amateur gamers that started in Tunisia
  • Our goal is to connect and bring together all people who want to play a sport
  • Our mission is to allow everyone to find a match every day near you
  • Our mission is to allow everyone to find a match every day near you
  • We believe everyone should be able to play safely
  • On-pitch violence will result in a an immediate ban on the game and the app

Take into account:

  • If you cannot play, withdraw at least 24 hours before the game, you will then be refunded the credits in your account which can be spent on another match
  • If the match is cancelled, you will be refunded the game credits to your account
  • We will cancel the game if the pitch administration cancels our reservation, if the weather is really bad(outdoor pitches) or if the number of players is insufficient for the match to take place accordingly(less than 80% of the squad planned total)
  • You must be 18 years or older to participate
  • You can pay directly by credit card or at office in your city
